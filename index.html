📋 Rules & regulations (reference only — not shown publicly)

⚽ General rules

👕
5-a-side — 5 players on pitch per team. Rolling subs, unlimited.
⏱
10-minute games — straight time, no stoppage time. Referee's whistle is final.
👧
Mixed gender — at least 2 female players on pitch at all times.
🚫
No sliding tackles — safety first. Sliding = free kick.
🚫
No heading — reduces collision risk on small courts.
🦶
Kick-ins instead of throw-ins. Ball stationary, taken within 5 seconds.
🧤
Goalkeeper — no punting. Rolling or throwing only.
🟨
Yellow card — 2-minute sin bin, team plays short.
🟥
Red card — player out for the rest of the tournament.

📊 Points system

✅
Win = 3 points
🤝
Draw = 1 point each
📊
Tiebreaker: Goal difference → Goals scored → Head-to-head → Penalty shootout

⚡ Penalty shootout (MLS-style)

🏃
MLS-style shootout — player starts at the 3/4 court line, dribbles in, must shoot before the D line.
🎯
3 kicks each, alternating. Sudden death if still level.
