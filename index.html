📋 Rules & regulations

⚽ General rules

👕
5-a-side — 5 players on pitch per team. Rolling subs, unlimited.
⏱
12-minute games — straight time, no stoppage time. Referee's whistle is final.
👧
Mixed gender — at least 2 female players on pitch at all times.
🚫
No sliding tackles — safety first. Sliding = free kick.
🚫
No heading — reduces collision risk on small courts.
🦶
Kick-ins instead of throw-ins. Ball stationary, taken within 5 seconds.
5️⃣
5-second rule on all restarts — kick-ins, corners, goal kicks, keeper distribution.
🧤
Goalkeeper — no punting. Rolling or throwing only. Cannot be scored on directly from a kick-in.
🟨
Yellow card — 2-minute sin bin, team plays short.
🟥
Red card — player out for the rest of the tournament. No replacement.
✝️
Spirit of the game — we play to honour God. Respect opponents, referees and teammates. Arguing with the referee = yellow card.

📊 Points system

✅
Win = 3 points
🤝
Draw = 1 point each
❌
Loss = 0 points
📊
Tiebreaker: Goal difference → Goals scored → Head-to-head → Penalty shootout

⚡ Knockout rules

🎯
If level after 12 mins — straight to penalties. No extra time.
🎯
Shootout — 3 kicks each, alternating. Sudden death if still level.

🔀 Mixed Cup draw

🎲
Players randomly drawn into 4 new teams after the SLC Cup group stage.
🧩
Each mixed team must have players from at least 2 different fellowships.
⚖️
Draw master ensures gender balance across all 4 teams.
